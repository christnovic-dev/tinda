# 🛒 TindaExpress 2026

**Cross-border eCommerce platform connecting China and the Democratic Republic of Congo**

A modern marketplace designed to simplify product sourcing, ordering, and delivery for customers in the DRC.

---

## 🌍 About the Project

**TindaExpress** is an e-commerce platform built to help customers in the Democratic Republic of Congo easily purchase products imported mainly from China.

The platform provides a digital marketplace where users can browse products, place orders, and track deliveries through a mobile application, while administrators and vendors manage operations through a web dashboard.

The system is designed to start as a **single-vendor store** and later scale into a **full multi-vendor marketplace**.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Our mission is to simplify international commerce between **China and Congo** by building a reliable platform that connects suppliers, vendors, and customers in a transparent and efficient way.

---

## 🏗 Platform Structure

TindaExpress is composed of three main components:

### 📱 Mobile Application
Customer-facing mobile app used to:
- Browse products
- Search and filter items
- Add products to cart
- Place orders
- Track deliveries

### 🖥 Backend System
The backend handles the core business logic of the platform including:
- Product management
- Order processing
- User authentication
- Vendor management
- Payment handling

### ⚙ Admin & Vendor Dashboard
Web interface used by administrators and vendors to manage the platform.

Capabilities include:
- Managing products
- Managing orders
- Monitoring sales
- Vendor management
- Platform configuration

---

## 🏪 Marketplace Model

The platform supports two operational modes:

### Single Vendor Mode
The platform owner sells products directly to customers.

### Multi Vendor Mode
Multiple vendors can register and sell their products on the marketplace.

The architecture is built to easily transition from single vendor to multi vendor as the platform grows.

---

## 🚀 Vision

TindaExpress aims to become a trusted e-commerce platform in the Democratic Republic of Congo by:

- Improving access to international products
- Simplifying logistics between China and Africa
- Supporting local vendors and entrepreneurs
- Providing transparent order tracking and delivery systems
